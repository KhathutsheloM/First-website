With over a decade of developing a mainstream for Venda Rap music . I am happy to say today we are here and more is still to come.

Contact me : cattermatic@gmail.com

My name is Magodi Khathutshelo well known by my stage name Catterlog, I come from a small village in Limpopo called Hamapila.

Passion for music started years ago , when i was still in primary and my inspiration came from local rappers as wel as international rappers. Due to unpreviledge background it took me more than six full years to find a proper studio and potray my art.

In 2014 i became a member of the group Young Stunnaz which was co-founded by myself and friends. We recorded our first mixtape together titled First Degree of Fame

The Song Shout Out being a hit song , it gained us exposure and thats when we got our first bookings , Young Stunnaz became a recognized crew in the Nzhelele rap game
